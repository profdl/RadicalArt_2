This site was created by Remko Scha (Institute of Artificial Art Amsterdam).

Some of the material presented here was compiled in the context of courses about algorithmic art and its art-historical and conceptual context, developed by Remko Scha for the Frank Mohr Institute, Groningen (2001-2003) and the MA New Media of the University of Amsterdam (2001-2005). Jos de Bruin and Jochem van der Spek made substantial contributions to these courses.

The term "radical art" is due to Van Lagestein.
